What
Features
Multi-scale optimization
One of the key features that sets Swan apart from other topology optimization toolboxes is the ability to design optimal materials at the micro scale, and reuse the obtained results to perform analyses at the macro level.
Optimization algorithms
Swan can solve topology optimization problems using various strategies, including density-based optimizers like Projected Gradient, MMA and IPOPT, as well as level-set methods such as SLERP, Projected SLERP and Hamilton-Jacobi.
Various constraints
The modular design of Swan allows the combination of several functions in order to define different optimization problems. Among the functions that can be used as constraints are compliance, volume, perimeter, non-self adjoint compliance and homogenized elasticity matrix.
The road ahead
We are constantly looking ahead and recruiting new contributors in order to keep expanding Swan's capabilities. Among the planned upcmoing features are multiphysics, 3D microstructural optimization, and many more.

FAQs

Who leads this?

Swan is Àlex Ferrer's brainchild. He started developing this toolbox as part of his PhD thesis, and its scope has since broadened. This code is currently used by the Composite and Advanced Materials for Multifunctional Structures research group at CIMNE, the International Center for Numerical Methods in Engineering.

Try it yourself

Swan is completely free and open-source, so download the code and start optimizing! Feel free to contact us if you want to add a new feature or are not quite sure how to tackle your problem.
